AutosportInternational
 autosport international is a four-day event motor-show that includes a two day dedicated trade show for industry professionals and a two day dedicated public show for motorsport fans   1    nit is held at the nec  in birmingham  united kingdom and operated by autosport media uk - part of motorsport network  usually in the second week of january  the 2018 show will be held on 11th - 14th january   its vital statistics include 27,000 trade visitors and 60,000 public visitors in attendance with over 5,000 overseas visitors and an 80  male demographic   all categories of track and off-road motor racing are represented  including formula one  btcc  24 hours of le mans and much  much more  the show also has an adrenaline packed live action arena where spectators can witness motorsport action and stunts up close and personal   the show has close links with many industry magazine titles  these publications inlclude autosport  motorsport news and f1 racing  who all actively participate at the show   industry bodies also have close affiliations entitling members to many discounts and offers  these include the motor sports association and the british racing and sports car club 2
